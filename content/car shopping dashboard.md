In the past, I used a spreadsheet version of this to help with car shopping. It's useful for understanding total costs of car + loan, monthly payments etc. I had AI code it up as a `streamlit` dash. 

Also included a simple gas/EV expenditure section for when you're arguing with friends about fuel vs electricity costs and need some hypothetical situations so you're not blindly talking over one another.

https://car-info-dash.streamlit.app
